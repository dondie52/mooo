Final Year Project

LMHTS

Livestock Management & Health Tracking System for smallholder cattle farmers in Botswana. Track animals, vaccinations, health events, breeding cycles, and generate BMC/BAITS compliance reports.

View Source on GitHub
🐄

Animal Management

Complete CRUD for cattle records with tag numbers, LITS tags, breeds, health status, and location tracking.

💉

Vaccination Tracking

Record vaccinations, track due dates, and monitor BMC coverage compliance with automated reminders.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Herd composition, vaccination trends, disease frequency analysis, and predictive health risk scoring.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Farmer, veterinarian, and admin roles with Row Level Security policies enforced at the database level.

📋

Compliance Reports

Generate vaccination certificates, animal traceability reports, and health summaries with CSV export.

🔔

Automated Alerts

Email reminders for upcoming vaccinations, overdue treatments, expected calvings, and coverage warnings.
